Living History
Our Origin

Xolos Ramírez: Guardians of the Living Pyramid

Founded by Fernando and Alexandra Ramirez, our kennel is the result of over 7 years of dedication to preserving the Xoloitzcuintle. For us, each dog is a "living pyramid" and a guardian of time with over 3,000 years of history.

International Lineage and Pedigree

We built our genealogy from scratch. Today, the Ramírez surname is a seal of quality on international blue pedigrees endorsed by the Mexican Kennel Federation (FCM), ensuring full traceability via microchip and tattoo on a Lineage NFT.

Our mission transcends borders: we export the culture and spiritual legacy of Mexico to families in Europe, Asia, and across the Americas.
